Business License Watermark: Essential Tool for Company Information Security

Why Add Watermarks to Business Licenses?

In business operations, business licenses are frequently required for various verifications and registrations. However, these documents contain crucial company information that could pose security risks if misused. Adding watermarks effectively indicates the intended use and prevents unauthorized usage, protecting your company's information security.

Common Use Cases

1. WeChat Official Account Verification

When submitting your business license for WeChat Official Account verification, it's recommended to add a watermark stating "For WeChat Official Account Verification Only, Invalid for Other Uses" to prevent document misuse.

2. Platform Registration

When registering on e-commerce or business service platforms, add platform-specific watermarks to ensure document exclusivity.

3. Business Cooperation

When providing business licenses to partners, add watermarks with project names or specific purposes to prevent documents from being used in other contexts.

  1. Use clear purpose statements in watermarks, like "For XX Verification Only"
  2. Choose appropriate watermark positions, consider using tile mode for better security
  3. Adjust opacity to maintain document readability
  4. Verify watermark effect and document completeness after processing

Security Notice

Important: When handling business licenses, please:

  • Use trusted tools to prevent information leakage
  • Safely store original documents, avoid excessive sharing
  • Regularly check the usage status of shared documents
